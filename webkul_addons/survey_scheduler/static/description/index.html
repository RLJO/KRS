Scheduled Survey with Follow-Up

Since surveys are an impartial method for effective decision making therefore what could be better than an automated system to conduct surveys. With the help of Odoo schedules survey, a defined survey will be sent on the Scheduled date. Also, this module will allow sending Follow-Up mail to the users.

Basic Features
  • Enables Scheduled Survey Feature.
  • Set a Scheduled date for Survey.
  • The survey will be sent on the Scheduled date.
  • Allows sending Follow-Up mail to the users.
  • Follow-Up may be taken at 'Once' or 'Repeatedly'.
  • Adds Authorised Users to Survey.
  • Manages Follow-up History.
