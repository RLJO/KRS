Product Wishlist

Add your favourite products to your wishlist, and move them anytime to your shopping cart.

Features:

  • Add feature of wishlist to your Shop. Allow customers to save their favourite products to wishlist and shop them anytime by moving products to cart with just a single click.
  • Remove hassle to browse your favourite product, again and again. Just add them to wishlist and easily move them to cart.
    • You can see 'added to wishlist' message when your product successfully added in wishlist, it helps you to remember your product that you are added in wishlist.
  • You can see 'added to wishlist' message when your product successfully added in wishlist, it helps you to remember your product that you are added in wishlist.
  • Like a product? Add your favourite product to your wishlist and avoid remebering your browing history.
  • Removal of product from wishlist and moving product(s) to cart is easy too.
  • Keep a track of popular products, which customers like and add them to their wishlist. See the number of customer who have added a particular product to their wishlist.
